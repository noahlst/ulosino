# Privacy Notice

The team at ULOSINO ('ULOSINO', 'our', 'us') takes your privacy seriously. When you use the ULOSINO web service ('the service'), you should consider the following notice - if you do not agree to the ULOSINO Privacy Notice, do not use the service.

---

## Data collection

ULOSINO does not collect personal information through the service.

When you access the service, you are downloading data from the server ('delivery software'). Most delivery software used by the service is open source and publicly available for copy or study.

Delivery software may collect:

- The date and time when you access the service;
- The web browser name (for example, Chrome) and version;
- The operating system name (for example, Windows) and version;
- The values of operating system accessibility and colour mode preferences, and;
- The operating system language display method (for example, right-to-left).

Delivery software does not collect:

- Internet Protocol (IP) address;
- Location or region information, and;
- Referring website, if applicable.

Data collected by delivery software is collected automatically in order to provide additional features and/or deliver the service without error (for example when using an older browser that may not support the requested feature).

Data collected by delivery software is not transmitted to ULOSINO.

---

## Tracking, advertising, and analytics

The service does not use trackers or advertising.

The service may collect automated performance analytics. These requests do not collect personal information and are used for product improvement purposes only.

---

## Request storage and cookies

The service does not store cookies, nor track you using cookies.

When using the service, the disk storage of the your computer may be used to store display preferences, provide features, or cache the service for offline availability. This data is stored on the computer until the request cache expires, or unless cleared manually.

## Security

The service is secured to the highest degree that ULOSINO can achieve.

Requests made through ULOSINO.com are sent using HTTPS/SSL and TLS. Additionally, the service uses a strict Content Security Policy to protect the service, when delivered to you, from XSS and code injection.

Requests, as handled by our service delivery partner Vercel, are GDPR and SOC2 compliant. <Link href="https://vercel.com/security">Learn more...</Link>
